About Us

Welcome to the ePass Management System! We are a team of enthusiastic interns working in the JaKeGa department of IT Jammu and Kashmir.

Our mission is to develop and enhance the ePass system, providing a seamless and efficient solution for managing travel permissions. As interns, we bring fresh ideas and a passion for innovation to contribute to the success of this project.

Feel free to explore our system, and if you have any questions or feedback, don't hesitate to reach out to us. Thank you for choosing ePass!
