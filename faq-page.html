Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is an ePass?

A: An ePass is a digital pass that allows individuals to streamline their travel process, providing a convenient and efficient way to manage travel permissions.

Q: How can I apply for an ePass?

A: To apply for an ePass, visit our "Apply Here" page and follow the provided instructions. You'll need to provide necessary details and supporting documents.

Q: Can I track the status of my ePass application?

A: Yes, you can easily track the status of your ePass application in real-time. Visit the "Track Status" page and enter the required information.
